Who are we?


Dream Travel is the leading classic and sports car tour operator. Established in 1996 we are the only classic and sports car tour operator to be both members of Association of British Travel Agents and the Association of Independent Tour Operators. Travel Destinations also hold an ATOL licence, so whichever of our exciting destinations you choose to go to, you can book safe in the knowledge that whatever happens your booking is financially protected and that you will receive the best advice and the highest level of customer service. ATOL is a government backed holiday scheme. See below for more details. All Travel Destinations staff have a background and training from within the travel industry. Customer service is always a priority. All our staff also visit & work at our events, which enables them to talk to you with a unique experience of what an event is like. Our speciality is Le Mans and we all have stories to tell from the Le Mans 24 Hours & the Le Mans Classic. All our bookings are made bespoke to your requirements.
